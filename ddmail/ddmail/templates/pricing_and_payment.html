Pricing and payment

Here you can find information regarding pricing and payment.

Pricing

20 Swedish kronor or 2 euro per month for every 1G of totalt storage. The most amount of disc space that you used during the 1 month period will be what you will be billed for.

Payment method

Payment is physical cash. Payment is done by putting your cash and payment token (randomly generated on our website) in an physical envelope and sending it to us. When we recive money we will generate a new payment token for your account.
